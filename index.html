Our Story

Welcome to NAMASTE, where we believe that every bite should be a warm welcome. Located in the heart of Rajarhat, Kolkata, we've been serving authentic, delicious food that brings people together.

Our mission is simple: to provide high-quality, flavorful meals at affordable prices. From our famous momos to our hearty rice combos, every dish is prepared with fresh ingredients and lots of love.

Our Promise

At NAMASTE, we're committed to maintaining the highest standards of food quality and hygiene. We use only fresh ingredients and traditional cooking methods to ensure every meal is both delicious and memorable.

Whether you're craving a quick snack or a full meal, our diverse menu has something for everyone. We take pride in being your go-to spot for comfort food in the neighborhood.
